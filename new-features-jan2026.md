## The Core Concept: "The Trusted Wellness Loop"

The goal is to move from a generic chatbot to a **Service-Oriented Assistant**. Instead of just giving information, it executes tasks (calculating, filtering, and linking).

### 1. User Profiling (The "Personalization Engine")

To meet Ashish’s requirement for low GI/GL or vegan options, the bot needs a **Dynamic Profile**:

* **Vitals:** Age, Weight, Height (for the BMI service).
* **Dietary Identity:** Vegan, Keto, Jain, Diabetic-friendly, etc.
* **Health Goals:** Muscle gain, Weight loss, PCOS management, Skin health.
* **Location:** To ensure the "Authentic Links" are shippable and relevant to the local market.

### 2. The Service Menu (Navneet’s 4 Pillars)

Using "Suggested Questions" (pills/chips) makes the UX frictionless:

* **BMI Calculator:** . The bot does the math and immediately pivots to a diet suggestion.
* **Custom Diet Tool:** Generates a meal plan based on the profile (e.g., "High protein, Vegan, Low GI").
* **Curated Marketplace:** This is the "10 best products" philosophy. Instead of 1,000 results, the bot says: *"Based on your goal for Marine Collagen, here is the #1 lab-tested brand available in your pincode."*
